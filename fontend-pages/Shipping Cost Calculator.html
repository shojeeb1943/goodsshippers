How Shipping Weight Is Calculated

straighten

Volumetric Formula

(L x W x H) / 5000

Calculated in centimeters. Carriers charge for the greater of actual vs. volumetric weight.

inventory_2

Example Calculation

A box weighing 5kg with dimensions 50cm x 40cm x 30cm.

Volumetric: (50x40x30)/5000 = 12kg

Chargeable Weight: 12kg
